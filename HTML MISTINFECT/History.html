How we came up with MIST INFECT

Bacteria are considered as one of the common reasons that affect one's health may it be a child, adult, or a senior. MGN building comprises the College of Nursing wherein experiments are being conducted and for the concern of the researchers, facilities such as laboratories may not be properly disinfected. The researchers decided to come up with a plan to lessen the spread of bacteria in MGN building and to improve the hygiene routine of its facilities through the installation of Mist Infect as a high coverage disinfectant.
